Wild-Type is a platform for hardware experiments. Color is our first generation of devices; it's a collection of 3 smartphones: Type I, Type II, and Type III.

Wild-Type Color is designed with irreverence—supporting a diversity of shapes, sizes, colors, and capabilities. Optimized for operating system flexibility, privacy, augmented reality, and extended battery life among other things, the devices are an adventurous departure from tradition.
